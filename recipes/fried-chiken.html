Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken pieces (legs, thighs, wings, breasts, or a combination)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  2. Pour buttermilk into a separate shallow dish.
  3. Dip each chicken piece into the buttermilk, coating completely, then roll in the flour mixture to coat.
  4. Place the coated chicken pieces on a plate or tray.
  5. Heat about 1/2-inch of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until it reaches 350Â°F.
  6. Carefully place 3-4 chicken pieces into the hot oil. Do not overcrowd the skillet.
  7. Fry the chicken for 8-10 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. Repeat with the remaining chicken pieces.
  8. Remove the fried chicken from the oil with a slotted spoon and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

Crunchy and delicious Fried Chicken, perfect for any occasion!
